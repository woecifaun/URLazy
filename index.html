Easy bookmarklet generator to switch between different environments of the same project.

How to

1
Fill all the sections for every environment you want to handle.
2
Drag one or many buttons to your personal bookmark.
3
Once on your dev page, click the prod bookmark to change the location of page to prod, keeping same path and query.

Use case

When working on an app having different environments like prod, demo, dev and so on… you constantly change part of the URL in order to compare the behaviors on different environments. This generator aims to improve your workflow by building bookmarklets you can place in the personal toolbar of your browser.

Note: You need to fill a line for every environment you want to replace by another one.
